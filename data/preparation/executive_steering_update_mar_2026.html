Executive Project Status Update

Project

Klang Valley East Metro - Package V3

Reporting date: 20 Mar 2026

Audience: Executive Steering Committee

1. Headline Position

Package V3 remains deliverable but is under increasing schedule pressure. Overall actual progress is 36.8% versus planned 39.0%, giving a schedule variance of -2.2%. Cost is currently manageable but forecast at completion has moved to RM 1.365 billion, driven mainly by Cheras North disruption, utilities diversion complexity, and prolonged interface coordination.

3. Key Messages for Steering Committee

3.1 What Changed Since February

  • Schedule slippage widened from -1.3% at end-February to -2.2% in mid-March.
  • Utility diversion completion for the Cheras North corridor has not yet been fully secured.
  • Interface design freeze was not achieved in full by end-March as originally targeted.
  • Forecast at completion increased from RM 1.352 billion to RM 1.365 billion.

3.2 What Is Still Going Well

  • Core viaduct production remains broadly recoverable if supply and weather stabilise.
  • Pandan Gateway Station is only marginally behind target.
  • Safety performance remains stable and no major quality trend has emerged.
