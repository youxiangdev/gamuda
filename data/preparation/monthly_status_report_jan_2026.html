Monthly Status Report

Project

Klang Valley East Metro - Package V3

Reporting month: January 2026

Prepared for: Project Delivery Leadership Team

1. Executive Summary

Package V3 closed January at overall actual progress of 31.5% against planned 32.0%. Overall project health remains Amber. Viaduct works continued to advance steadily, but Cheras North enabling and utilities activities were slower than planned due to unresolved authority comments on diversion methodology and limited night work access.

Commercially, the package remains within manageable range but early signals show pressure on utilities relocation and interface-related design coordination. Quality performance was broadly acceptable, although NCR-014 on pier reinforcement alignment remains under corrective action.

4. Achievements This Month

  • Completed pier cap works at six viaduct locations.
  • Substantially completed bored piling for Pandan Gateway Station.
  • Installed temporary traffic decking at one crossing to unlock future station activities.
  • Closed out most structural shop drawing comments for Pandan Gateway concourse zone.

5. Key Issues

5.1 Utilities and Access

The diversion package at Cheras North Station did not progress at the planned rate because the authority requested additional method statement clarification for one crossing. This restricted access to the permanent works zone and delayed downstream station activities.

5.2 Quality

NCR-014 was raised following a pier reinforcement alignment discrepancy. The site team completed immediate corrective work and expects closure in February after QAQC verification.

5.3 Design Interface

The systems interface package remains partially approved only. Civil and systems comments were resolved internally, but client-side comments on cable route access and room handover sequence are still outstanding.
